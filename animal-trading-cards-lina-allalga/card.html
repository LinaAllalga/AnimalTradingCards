Panda

An adult panda can eat up to 38 kilos of bamboo per day!

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Average Length: 1.2 to 1.9 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: Temperate Forest

The giant panda lives in a few mountain ranges in central China, mainly in Sichuan, but also in neighbouring Shaanxi and Gansu. As a result of farming, deforestation, and other development, the panda is a vulnerable species.
